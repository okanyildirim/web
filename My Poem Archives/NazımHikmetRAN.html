Nazım Hikmet RAN

Nâzım Hikmet Ran commonly known as Nâzım Hikmet was born in 15 January 1902. He was not only poet and novelist, playwright, director screenwriter, and memoirist. He was called for the "lyrical flow of his statements" or romantic communist" and "romantic revolutionary", he was repeatedly accused of his political beliefs and spent much of his adult life in prison or in exile. His poetry has been translated into more than fifty languages. He was died in 3 June 1963.

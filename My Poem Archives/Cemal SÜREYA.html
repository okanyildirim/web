Cemal SÜREYA

Cemâl Süreya was born in 1931 and died 1990. He was both poet and writer one of the most revered figures in Turkish literature, and known for his loves, passions and relationships. His pen name is Cemâlettin Süreyya Seber. He successfully represented modernist Turkish poem with his style and content.
